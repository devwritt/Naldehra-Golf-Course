About Naldehra

Nestled amidst majestic deodar forests near Shimla, Naldehra Golf Course is one of the oldest and most scenic golf courses in India. Situated at approximately 2200 meters above sea level, the course offers breathtaking Himalayan views and a unique golfing experience.
